Positive Score (More than 0)

  • A positive sentiment score indicates that the summary conveys a tone of optimism, happiness, or success. The language used is likely to include uplifting words or phrases that evoke positive emotions like joy or hope.
  • Such scores often suggest that the content has a lighthearted or encouraging feel, highlighting achievements, positive experiences, or favorable outcomes.

Negative Scores (Less than 0)

  • A negative sentiment score suggests that the summary carries a tone of dissatisfaction, sadness, or frustration.
  • The text may include words or phrases that express negative emotions such as disappointment, anger, or sorrow.
  • The scores typically indicate a focus on unpleasant events, challenges, or negative experiences, often reflecting a more critical or somber perspective
